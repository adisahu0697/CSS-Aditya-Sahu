Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • Bacon
  • Cheddar cheese slices
  • Butter
  • Tomato slices
  • White bread slices

Instructions:

1. Preheat a skillet or griddle over medium heat.

2. Cook bacon until crispy, then set aside.

3. Spread butter on one side of each bread slice.

4. Place a slice of cheese on the unbuttered side of a bread slice, followed by tomato slices, bacon, and another slice of cheese. Top with another bread slice, butter side facing out.

5. Place the sandwich in the skillet or griddle and cook until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, flipping once.

6. Repeat with remaining ingredients to make additional sandwiches.
